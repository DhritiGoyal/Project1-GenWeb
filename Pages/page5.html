my understanding and reflection
This week's reading got me thinking about the control a designer has. The way the constants are decided, may restrict a "pattern follower". Also the concept of rules made in silence is interesting. I believe this is also an act of controlling. It could also be seen as a form of manipulation is a more dense context.
How can designers practice this power of creating rules in silence for a positive impact? What happens to the results, when they dont follow the rules? Are those results useless or perhaps offer something even more interesting?
