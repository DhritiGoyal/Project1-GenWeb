my understanding and reflection

The language in this reading was very specific. The term "play" is interesting as it is often associated with childishness and frivolity. However to compare a cmoputer to a toy, creates a completely new meaning and purpose for the machine. Furthermore, it is interesting to thing of certain playful activities and removing them from their obligatory "place and time". For instnace, what if toys were placed in a court room? People have formed certain ways of living, that have become the norm now, anything outside that is considered useless, and to put it in simpler words unnecessary and stupid. However, looking at it from a design perspective could produce some interesting results.
