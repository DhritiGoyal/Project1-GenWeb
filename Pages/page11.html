Quotes I found interesting from the reading
:: NFTs and The Readymade Reversed

"Duchamp gave sculpture over to information on two registers: He desublimated matter by suspending it between two names, shovel and shovel abd sculpture; and he abandoned objects for discourse. Desublimation and abandonment"(page 3)

"Duchamp used the category of art to liberate materiality from commodifiable form; the NFT deploys the category of art to extract private property from freely available information."(page 4)

"The relation between matter and property is political. Art can function as a technology of extraction, or it can, as Duchamp's work did, destabilize the movement of matter's becoming-property in ways that are historically specific and aesthetically engrosing." (page 4)

"The NFT is a social contract that values property over material experience. That contract can be broken."(page 4)
